WHAT IS BRICK HILL REWRITTEN?

Brick Hill Rewritten is a fan-made revival of the original Brick Hill platform (2017-2023), rebuilt from the ground up to preserve and continue its legacy. Our goal is to bring back everything players loved — avatar customization, item trading, user-created games, clans, and forums — in a modern, stable, and community-driven environment.

This is not an official Brick Hill product. It is an independent, non-profit project maintained by passionate fans who want to keep its spirit alive.

SAFE & MODERATED

We take safety seriously. Brick Hill Rewritten features active moderation, community reporting tools, and strict rules to ensure everyone — regardless of age — can enjoy the platform without worry. Our volunteer moderation team is dedicated to keeping the environment friendly and welcoming for all.

A Note for Parents

Brick Hill Rewritten is a free, non-commercial fan project designed to be enjoyable for players of all ages. We do not collect personal data beyond what is strictly necessary for account creation, we do not run advertisements, and there are no microtransactions or pay-to-win mechanics of any kind.

Our platform is actively moderated by volunteer staff who review reported content and enforce our community guidelines. Players can block or report others at any time. We encourage parents to explore the platform alongside their children and to reach out with any concerns.

If you have questions about your child's account, our rules, or how the platform works, please visit our dedicated parents page below.
